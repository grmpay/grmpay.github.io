How do I make a payment?

We are excited that you have made the decision to donate to Great Redemption Ministries!.

Please follow the instructions below to make a One-time Gift or Repeated Gift through our secure, encrypted giving site. Our online giving system uses high grade 256-bit SSL encryption, the strongest level of Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) supported by web standards. By activating your tithe.ly account you can access your giving history or make changes to your giving at any time.

One-time Giving
 1. Click here (No login is required for a one-time donation)
2. Enter desired amount (Must be at least $5 as required by tithe.ly)
3. Select appropriate designation fund from the "Give to" drop-down menu.
4. Enter required card details, email address and full name.
5. If you decide to further assist the church's finances, you can toggle "Cover card processing fees" NB: This adds a minimal fee to your donation amount.
6. You can also decide to automate this donation for next time by toggling "Setup automated giving", and selecting how often you want to automatically donate.
7. Click the large give button below the form to send your donation.
8. You eill receive a personalized email receipt confirmation.

Repeated Giving
In order to repeatedly give to Great Redemption Ministries and keep track of donations, you will need to create a tithe.ly account through GRMpay. If you already have an account, please login to proceed. If you do not have an account, please follow the instructions below to create one and make a donation.

1. On the main GRMpay site, click on the appropriate designation fund you want to make a donation under. (Tithe in this instance)
2. You will be redirected to the Great Redemption Ministries tithe.ly page for the specified designatiion fund. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the upper right portion of the page.
3. Click on the "Create Account" button to set up your new tithe.ly account to interface with GRMpay.
4. Fill in the form to create your new account. Enter your first and last names, email address, a secure password and a 4-digit pin. Take note of the 4 digit pin as this will be used to quickly login in whenever you want to access your tithe.ly account. Click "Submit" when you are satisfied with the provided information.
5. Enter your newly created 4-digit pin to login to tithe.ly
6. In order to make a repeated donation, you'd need to add a credit or debit card. To do this, click on the circled green plus icon in the upper right of the screen.
7. Fill in your card details accurately and click on "Add Card" to proceed. You are welcome to repeat this step to add multiple credit/debit cards.
8. The next screen you are presented with is where the donation happens. Firstly, cross-check to ensure that your donation is going to the appropriate designation funds. In this case, we wanted to make a tithe payment, so the listed designation fund is correct. After the quick verification, enter in the amount (in dollars) you'd like to donate to Great Redemption Ministries. Also, select the right credit/debit card you'd want to charge for the donation. Lastly, decide if you want to further assist the church's finances, by toggling "Cover card processing fees". NB: This adds a minimal fee to your donation amount. You can also decide to setup an automate giving using the credit/debit card you selected.
9. For this tutorial, we decided to pay a tithe amount of $100 from our debit card (blurred out), and also decided to cover card processing fees. This included an additonal $3.30 to our donation amount, bringing the total to be cahrd on our debit card: $103.30. After confirming the donation information is correct, click on "Pay $AMOUNT" to proceed.
10. Click on "OK" to confirm that you want to make a donation to Great Redemption Ministries.
11. Upon clicking the ok button, you are notified if your donation was successful or not, and offered an option to "Return Home". Click "Return Home" to be redirected to GRMpay
12. Upon redirect, you are thanked with a brief message from Great Redemption Ministries. Click on "RETURN TO GRM" if you'd like to be redirected to the official website of Great Redemption Ministries. Alternatively, you can click on the GRMpay logo to be redirected to the GRMpay home page.
13. Within a few minutes, check your email inbox for a personalized transaction receipt from the Great Redemption Ministries Finance Team, notifying you of an approved and verified transaction through GRMpay. If you have any questions regarding the transaction, reach out to GRM's tech support team via email at: grmmedia16@gmail.com


FAQs

Take a look through these frequently asked questions about giving electronically with Tithe.ly. If your question isn’t answered, you can contact Great Redemption Ministries Tech Support Team for further details.

Who recommends Tithe.ly?
After a thorough search of digital giving platforms, our Stewardship Committee recommended Tithe.ly, which was endorsed by our Finance Committee, and blessed by the Church Council. Tithe.ly has also been endorsed by the ELCA as a preferred vendor for digital giving.

Is my Credit/Debit card information secure?
Yes! Tithe.ly takes data and payment information security very seriously. All sensitive financial information is stored with bank level security. All information transmission to Tithe.ly’s banking partner and on our site is done via an encrypted HTTPS (SSL) connection, and stored by the banking partner to PCI DSS Level 1 compliant standards.

How will my data be used?
Your personal data will never be sold, traded, or given out to third parties. Your information will be stored only for the purposes of processing your gifts and providing you with giving statements. Read the Tithe.ly privacy policy.

How will I know when my credit, debit, or ACH is charged?
Anytime you are charged you will receive an email notification from Tithe.ly.

Can I give to different funds?
Yes. On the second line of the Giving form, under the label “Give to” you may select to direct your donation to either the General Fund, or one of our other funds. If you would like to give to more than one fund, click the “ Add Gift” button on the next line to add another gift to your transaction.

Will I get a receipt?
Absolutely! As soon as you give via Tithe.ly you will instantly receive an email with the details of your transaction. All of your online giving history is also tracked and available within Tithe.ly interface, giving you instant access to check your records. Click the button to access your account details. You will still receive a giving statement from the church as usual with your total giving amounts.

What if I accidentally type the wrong amount?
If you make a mistake while entering a gift, contact the Great Redemption Ministries Tech Support Team and we can refund the incorrect amount and set up another donation for the correct amount. Refunds may take up to 7 business days to appear back in your account.

What Giving methods are available?
You can give using your VISA, MasterCard, Discover, or AMEX. ACH bank transfer (aka eCheck) donations are also supported by Tithe.ly church giving. Please consider giving by ACH as it incurs a smaller processing fee, ensuring more of your donation goes to supporting our ministry.

How do I change or update my payment method?
Click the button to access your account details. Click “Payment Methods” to add or remove payment methods. Click the buttons at the bottom of the screen to switch between your credit/debit cards and your bank account (ACH) details.

What is “Cover card processing fees”?
All electronic giving involves processing fees. These fees help cover the cost of processing each electronic transaction, and maintaining a secure and safe environment for your transactions. By selecting the “Cover card processing fees” option, you increase your donation by the amount of the fee, ensuring St. Peter’s receives your full intended gift amount. To reduce processing fees, consider giving by ACH bank transfer (aka eCheck).

How do I change my auto recurring giving amount?
Click the button to access your account details. Click “Recurring Gifts”. On this screen, you’ll see your existing recurring gifts. Click the red trash icon to delete your existing current gift that you’d like to change. Click the button and return to the Home page of the app. Then setup a new auto recurring gift for the desired amount.

Do I have to have a Tithe.ly account to give?
When giving, registering, or paying via the Tithe.ly web giving form you do NOT have to create a Tithe.ly account to make one-time or recurring donations. Simply enter your payment details and click the Give button.

What benefits are there to setting up a Tithe.ly account?
By creating an account, you’ll be able to securely store your payment method (so you don’t have to enter it each time), setup and manage recurring donations, see your online giving history, and manage your account yourself 24/7.

What about Paypal?
As of 08/2017, Great Redemption Ministries has retracted its PayPal account. All transactions and donations are processed through GRMpay using tithe.ly. Please contact the Great Redemption Ministries Tech Support Team if you'd like to learn more about the reasoning behind the transition.
